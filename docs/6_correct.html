DeCentering

6. Health and Medical Space

Navigating a racist, fatphobic, and sexist system with a marginalized body that is facing the effects of weathering from generational and present traumas.

Themes:

History of Medicine

How do we reconcile with the way that medicine is held up and necessary in our society with its racist and ableist histories?

Labor of Advocacy

How is advocacting for one's own health and autonomy in medical space a form of assimilative labor for intersectionally marginalized individuals?

Commodification of Health

How has health become a privilege that is harder to access for intersectionally marginalized individuals?
