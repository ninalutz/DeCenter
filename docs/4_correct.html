DeCentering

4. Careers and Professional Lives

Professions uphold white supremacy culture and hierarchies -- causing abuse and ostracization of intersectionally marginalized workers. These social and professional costs of navigating workplaces, career arcs, and abuse can upend careers and lives.

Themes:

What roles are intersectionally marginalized peoples limited to and told to "settle for"? How is this related to invisiblized labor?

How are WOC asked to fall on their old sword and to save workplaces and white colleagues?

How do gatekeeping and professionalism intersect?

Why are "white women of color" so harmful within workplaces and especially in fields like academia?
