DeCentering

3. Transportation and Movement

Lack of autonomy and safety through movement of space and time. The labor of navigating rural, suburban, and urban spaces as a person with a marginalized body whilst under the eye of kyriarchy and white violence.

Themes:

How does movement through space and time serve as autonomy, as self-care, as self-determination, as dignity and humanity?

How are the unwritten codes of conduct in suburban, "safer", white dominated spaces harmful to intersectionally marginalized individuals?
