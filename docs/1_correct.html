DeCentering

1. Social Media and Art

Examining the ecosystem of trauma, content, and policing and its effects in marginalized lives on and offline.

Themes:

Disconnection

How do you navigate and build worth and internal humanity when you see yourself in traumatizing media and how the world sees you as not worthy and not human?

Scale of dehumanization and violence

Opression, brutality, and violence are now at a scale of 24/7 visibility? What does this mean for intersectionally marginalized people and their interactions with those who do not see themselves in this violence?

Currency of quantification of oppressive acts

To what extent has White mainstream culture been able to co-opt canceling to police intersectionally marginalized peoples?
